## About Archive-35

Archive-35 is a fine art photography archive by Wolf Schram, a photographer with 25 years of experience across 55+ countries. The brand, "The Restless Eye," specializes in landscape, wildlife, urban, and architectural photography.

## For Developers & AI Agents

- **MCP Server**: `.well-known/mcp/server.json` describes our tool interface
- **API Endpoint**: `GET /api/license/gallery` with filters for subject, mood, location, orientation
- **Structured Data**: Every page has Schema.org JSON-LD
- **Machine-Readable**: `llms.txt` and `llms-full.txt` for LLM consumption
- **Sitemaps**: `sitemap.xml` and `sitemap-images.xml`
