## Rules I'm Following

1. **Lead with people, not technology** — Every cover letter, every interview answer
2. **Problem→Product→Result** — The framework for everything
3. **Quality over volume** — Better to apply to 5 right roles than 50 wrong ones
4. **Track everything** — Can't improve what I don't measure
5. **Iterate the template** — Change the opening hook, measure the difference
6. **Network > Apply** — Referrals convert higher than cold applications
7. **Stay authentic** — Servant leadership isn't a pitch, it's who I am
