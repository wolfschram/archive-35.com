## 4. Important Notes

- **CP 575 Notice:** The IRS will also mail a CP 575 confirmation letter to the address on file. Keep both the digital PDF and the mailed letter.
- **IRS Database Update:** It can take up to 2 weeks for the EIN to be fully registered in all IRS systems. If a third party (like a bank) cannot verify the EIN immediately, wait and try again.
- **One EIN Per Day:** The IRS limits one EIN per responsible party per day.
- **Keep This Number Secure:** Treat your EIN like your SSN for business purposes. Do not share it publicly.
- **Cannot Be Cancelled:** Once issued, an EIN cannot be cancelled. You can close the associated business account by writing to the IRS.
